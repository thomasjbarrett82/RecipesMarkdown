# Pound Cake

Source: @bdylanhollis

## Ingredients

- 1 lb butter
- 1 lb sugar
- 1 lb eggs (~9)
- 1 lb flour

## Prep

- Heat oven to 325 F
- Grease and flour a bundt cake pan (or 8x8)

## Directions

- Cream butter and sugar
- Mix in eggs thoroughly
- Stir in flour
- Add to cake pan
- Bake for 80-100 mins
- Let cool before serving with fruit and ice cream/whipped cream
