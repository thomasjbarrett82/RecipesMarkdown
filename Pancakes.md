-   2 eggs

-   1 cup buttermilk

-   1 cup whole milk

-   ¼ cup shortening, melted

-   2 cups flour

-   1 tsp. baking soda

-   1 tsp. baking powder

-   1/3 cup sugar

-   1 tsp. salt

**PREP**

-   Heat griddle to 375 F

**DIRECTIONS**

-   In a small bowl, beat the eggs well

-   Fold into the other ingredients, but don’t over mix

-   Fry on the griddle until golden brown
