# Korean Marinade or Dipping Sauce

Source: DJU

## Ingredients

- 1 cup soy sauce
- 2 tbsp gochugaru flakes
- 3-4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 scallions, chopped
- 1/4 cup red onion, minced
- 1-2 jalapenos, minced
- 2 tbsp maple syrup
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp mirin
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 1/2 tsp turmeric

## Directions

- Whisk all ingredients in bowl
- Use as meat marinade or a dipping sauce
