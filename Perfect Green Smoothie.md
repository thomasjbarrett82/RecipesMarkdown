# Perfect Green Smoothie

## Ingredients

- 1 banana, sliced
- 1 cup tropical fruit
- 3 pitted dates
- 1/8 cup raw almonds
- 2 cups baby spinach
- 1 Â½ cups water

## Prep

- If desired, combine all ingredients in a wide-mouth mason jar
- Freeze for later use

## Directions

- Add all ingredients to a strong blender
- Blend until smooth
