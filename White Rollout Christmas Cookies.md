- 1 cup sugar

- 1 cup butter

- 2 eggs

- 3 tbsp. sour cream

- 1 tsp. baking soda

- 3 cups flour

- Â½ tsp. salt

- 1 tsp. vanilla

## PREP

- Heat oven to 350 F

## DIRECTIONS

- In medium bowl, cream sugar and butter

- Add in eggs, sour cream, baking soda and mix well

- Fold in flour, salt, and vanilla

- Chill in fridge for 30 minutes

- Roll out and cut into shapes

- Bake until light brown
