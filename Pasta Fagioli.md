-   1 can Northern beans

-   1# ground beef, browned & drained

-   1 onion, chopped

-   2 stalks celery, chopped

-   3 cloves garlic, minced

-   2 cans beef broth

-   2 cans diced tomato

-   ½ tsp black pepper

-   ¼ tsp cayenne

-   ¼ tsp hot sauce

-   1 tsp basil

-   3 cups cooked pasta

**DIRECTIONS**

-   Combine all ingredients except pasta in crockpot

-   Cook on LOW for 8-10 hours or HIGH for 4-5 hours

-   Add pasta and serve
