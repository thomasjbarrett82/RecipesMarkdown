# 

## Ingredients

- 3 avocados

- 3 cloves garlic, minced

- ½ red onion, minced

- 1 jalapeno, minced

- ¼ cup cilantro, chopped

- 2 T. lemon juice

- ½ t. cumin

- ½ t. chili powder

- ½ t. black pepper

- Kosher salt

## Directions

- Pit and mash the avocado in a bowl

- Stir in the remaining ingredients

- Salt to taste
