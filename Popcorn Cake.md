- 1 lb. marshmallows

- ½ cup oil

- ½ cup butter

- 20 cups popped corn

- 1 cup peanuts

- 1 cup M&M’s

- Food coloring

## DIRECTIONS

- Microwave marshmallows, butter, and oil

- Go 30 seconds at a time until melted together

- Mix remaining ingredients together

- Pour into greased angel food pan

- Let cool and tip out when done
