# Rice Corn Bread

Source: @bdylanhollis

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups scalded milk
- 1 1/2 cups cornmeal
- 2 tbsp. butter
- 1 egg
- 1 cup cooked mushy rice
- 4 tsp. baking powder
- pinch of salt

## Prep

- Heat oven to 400 F
- Grease 8x8 baking dish

## Directions

- Pour milk over cornmeal & butter
- Cool
- Add beaten egg, rice, baking powder, salt
- Pour into dish
- Bake for 30 mins
- Let cool before serving with butter and honey
